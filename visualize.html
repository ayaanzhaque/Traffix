What are the map's functions?

This application is mainly meant for friends of those who are going through depression so they can accurately see specifically what their friend is dealing with. Additionally, therapists and doctors can also use our algorithm to better understand their patients especially when their phrases are hard to distinguish and cryptic.

Functions

(Created Pins) Left Hold: Drag
On a created pin, hold down and move the pin around to where you prefer it most.

(Created Pins) Right Click: Delete
Right click on a created pin to delete it.

(Both Pins) Left Click: Info Window
Left click on any pin to bring up a window of its information.

(Existing Pins) Right Click: Switch Pin Type
Right click on a pre-existing pin to switch to another pin type (e.g. Stop to Yield).

(Overall map) Reset & Save:
Click the reset button to reset your map, and the save button to save your design.

(Overall map) Download & Finalize:
Click this button to finalize your design and download a png of your product.

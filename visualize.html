Visualize

Envision your proposals for road infrastructure changes by experimenting with our interactive map, which will depict how traffic flow is improved.

Instructions

Map Legend
# Name Sign Meaning
1 Stop Sign Enter coordinates for a point where you would like to add a stop sign.
2 Yield Sign Enter coordinates for a point where you would like to add a yield sign.
3 Speed Limit Enter coordinates for a speed limit sign. Please specify the original speed on the sign and what you want to change it to.
4 Traffic Light Enter coordinates for a point where you would like to add a traffic light.
5 Lanes Enter two coordinates for the start and stop of where you want a new lane. To ensure accuracy right now, please ensure this road is linear and along a listed road.

Functions and Controls
# Name Compatible Pin Type Action Definition
1 Drag pin User-created Pins Left Hold On a created pin, hold down and move the pin around to where you prefer it most.
2 Delete Pin User-created Pins Right Click On a created pin, hold down and move the pin around to where you prefer it most.
3 Activate Infographic Window User-created Pins, Existing Pins Right click Right click on a pre-existing pin to switch to another pin type (e.g. Stop to Yield).
4 Switch Pin Type Existing Pins Right click Right click on a pre-existing pin to switch to another pin type (e.g. Stop to Yield).
5 Reset Map Overall Map Button at Bottom Click the reset button to reset your map.
6 Save Map Overall Map Button at Bottom Click the save button to save your design.
7 Download & Finalize Overall Map Button at Bottom Click this button under the proposal section to finalize your design and download a png of your product.
